## Internal Growth Paths

- Home mini check-in routes users to `/start?emotion=...`.
- Feeling and moment pages route to `/techniques/[slug]`, `/send`, `/blueprint`, `/find-help`, and `/companion`.
- `/today` creates the daily retention loop and shareable Garden/challenge milestones.
- `/share/[token]` handles safe Calm Card previews and redirects recipients toward the relevant tool or reset.

## Copy Rules

- Use: clinician-informed, evidence-framed, wellness companion, self-guided emotional first aid, not a substitute for professional care.
- Avoid: diagnosis, treatment, cure, guaranteed outcomes, medical advice, medication guidance, 100% private unless technically true for that feature.
